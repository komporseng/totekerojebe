Da Brat Biography

Da Brat was born on 14 April 1974 in Joliet, IL. Funkdafied, first female solo rapper to release a platinum-selling song. She also worked with stars such as Lil Wayne and Kelly Rowland. She began Co-hosting the Rickey Smiley Morning Show in 2015. She was featured on the top-five single “Sock It 2 Me” by Missy Elliot. Born to Shawntae Harris, she played basketball and ran high school track.
