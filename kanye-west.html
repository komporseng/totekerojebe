Ye popularly known as Kanye West is an American, who is a popular rapper, songwriter, singer, record producer, fashion designer, and entrepreneur by profession. He has achieved incredible heights in his career and has earned multiple awards and a marvelous net worth of about $7 billion. He rose to fame as a hip-hop musician and as a producer for a record label called “Roc-A-Fella Records” in 2000.

He is the inventor of a popular hip-hop trend called chipmunk soul sampling style and has explored diverse musical elements on his albums. His first studio album titled “The College Dropout” was launched in 2004,  and was highly admired. He is also the founder of a record label called “Good Music”.

Some of his notable albums are 2004 “The College Dropout”, 2005 “Late Registration, 2007 “Graduation”, 2008 “808s & Heartbreak”, 2010 “My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy”, 2013 “Yeezus” and many more. His album “Graduation” got a nomination for eight Grammy Awards and the single of the album titled “Stronger” earned a Grammy Award in the category of Best Rap Solo Performance.

He has been working as a musician since 1990. He has been nominated for the “Grammy Award” 69 times and has won it 21 times during his career.

Kanye West Wiki/Biography

Born on 8 June 1977, Kanye West’s age is about 45 years as of 2022. He was brought up and raised in a well-settled family in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. He is an American by nationality and has his belief in Christianity religion. He completed his early schooling at Polaris School.

After that, he enrolled himself at Chicago State University to study English. He also attended the American Academy of Art, Chicago in 1997 to learn painting. Since his childhood, he had a great interest in drawing and music and wanted to establish his career in the same field. In seventh grade, he started creating his musical compositions. He left college to pursue his musical career. His zodiac sign is Gemini.
