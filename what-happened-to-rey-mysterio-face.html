What Happened To Rey Mysterio Face?

We are happy to inform the fans that nothing has happened to Rey Mysterio’s face. There had been a rumor going around that he had lost his eye in an “Eye for an Eye” match.

However, the match was just for entertainment purposes only and Rey did not lose his eyes. His face is also in good condition regardless of him wearing his mask during his matches.

Due to Rey’s Lucha Libre origins in Mexico, luchadors are required to wear masks during matches. All luchadors over the globe see it as sacrosanct.

To continue and respect the history and legacy of Mysterio’s ancestors, he always wears his mask during his matches. There have been several instances where his mask has come off, but he is consistent with it and unless he is a part of some gig, he always wears his mask during his fights.

Despite competing in WCW without his mask for a time, Rey has never performed in WWE without it.
