Table of Biography

Koren Grieveson is a well-known chef, reality television celebrity, and former US Army soldier. Koren Grieveson’s cooking abilities attracted the attention of the media, and she turned a passion into a career. She was a member of the army for nine years and has worked as a cook for more than two decades. She is widely identified as one of America’s most affluent chefs due to her expertise and culinary talents.  Grieveson has managed a few stations and has worked as a cook, thanks to her culinary expertise.

Early Life

On November 4, 1971, Koren Grieveson was born in Luanda, Angola, in the United States. As of now, she is 51 years old and she holds an American by her nationality. She has a sun sign of Scorpio and belongs to the English-American ethnicity. Her father worked in the poultry industry, which necessitated his family’s constant movement. As a result, she and her sisters grew up in various parts of the globe. Before moving to the United States, she spent parts of her youth and young adulthood in Brazil, England, and Iran. After that, the family settled in Glastonbury, Connecticut in the end.

Her mother, like many of her classmates, gave her her first cooking instruction. Grieveson claims that her mother was a fantastic chef and that they ate a lot of chicken. She was exposed to a range of cuisines as a result of her numerous travels, which aided her in creating her culinary viewpoint. Talking about her education, she completed graduation from ‘The Culinary Institute of America.

Professional Career

Military career

She said in an interview with the ‘Food & Wine magazine that she joined the US Army because she wanted to drive the jeeps that the army employed at the time. Previous to her enlistment, though, they had abandoned the jeeps in favor of Humvees. When she initially spoke with a recruiter, she was 17 years old. Her mother warned her that she would be forced to serve in the military for eight years. Despite that, she joined the military and enjoyed it there. And after eight-year, she quit the military peacefully.

Culinary Career

She decided to work in the hotel business when she left the army. One of her earliest jobs in New York was delivering food and beverages to rock and roll bands. Lollapalooza, the Eagles, and the Rolling Stones are just a few of the legendary musical acts she’s worked with. She afterward spoke with her father, who counseled her to pursue a job. She went on to the Culinary Institute of America in New York after that. Following her graduation, she successfully established a great reputation in the industry.

She worked at Aqua, a now-defunct restaurant in San Francisco, during her time there. She started cooking at Spruce after moving to Chicago, Illinois. Additionally, she worked as a sous cook at celebrity chef and mentor Paul Kahan’s renowned restaurant Blackbird from 1998 until 2003. She also served as the chef de cuisine at Avec, a Mediterranean-themed wine bar that earned a lot of honors and is located adjacent to Blackbird. Moreover, she was asked to make meals for a “healthy” children’s festival at the White House in 2010.

More About Culinary Career

She returned to New York in 2012 and spent her initial days there living in the house of Prune chef Gabrielle Hamilton. Furthermore, she became the executive chef at Resto, a Belgian restaurant, in 2015. Before Grieveson, the kitchen has been led by notable chefs such as Ryan Skeen, Bobby Helen, and Preston Clarke. She decided to make small alterations after taking over while maintaining the restaurant’s general essence.

Resto has earned a reputation as a meat-centric bistro. Grieveson had no desire to modify this, but she did want her clients to be able to dine there two or three times a week without feeling stuffed. She created her harissa, which went perfectly with the mussels they were cooking. She also utilized spices and veggies to enhance the flavor of various recipes without overpowering them.

Awards

Talking about her awards and recognitions, she has received some of them. During her stint at Avec, she received a James Beard Award for Best Chef Great Lakes. She was named the Best New Chef by Food & Wine magazine in 2008.

Relationship Status

In 2012, she started dating Anne Burrell, a fellow chef, and host of the Food Network show “Secrets of a Restaurant Chef”. Later, Annie announced her engagement to Grieveson in a tweet on December 31. However, Annie married Stuart Claxton after her engagement ended with Koren.

Till now, Koren Grieveson is not a part of any kind of controversy nor got in rumor. Moreover, she focuses on her personal and professional life and improves her career as a chef.
