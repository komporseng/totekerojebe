Ryan Coogler is the American filmmaker known for directing the MCU movie Black Panther and its sequel. With his exceptionally well filmmaking skills, Coogler has been the recipient of many awards including Golden Globe and Academy Awards.

Who Is Ryan Coogler Wife?

Ryan Cooglers's wife is in the showbiz industry like himself. The Black Panther director Coogler is married to Zinzi Evans, a screenwriter, and producer. Sharing similar interests and close bond has strengthened their bond.

Zinzi Evans became Ryan Cooglers's wife in 2016. However, there are no official words on the marriage from the couple. Did you know? Zinzi and Ryan were high school sweethearts who were together since their young days.

The love has never faded for Coogler and his spouse Evans. They are still the same lovey-dovey couple who met each other in college days. Together the husband-wife duo are the parents of two now.

Zinzi Evans Bought Ryan Coogler A Software To Write Scripts

Undeniably Ryan Coogler's wife is a supportive partner. As they say, behind every successful man, there is a hand of woman, Ryan has been able to achieve such success because of his wife.

The unwavering support and dedication Evans displayed during the struggle days was unreal. Zinzi even bought him the software used to write scripts while in college.

"I was broke, playing football on the little scholarship money. And my wife scrapped together some cheese and bought me Final Draft, which is the software that you write your movies on. And she got me that."

The fact that Zinzi gifted him such a valuable tool led Coogler to pursue his dream. Today every success Ryan has is due to the support of his loving wife.
