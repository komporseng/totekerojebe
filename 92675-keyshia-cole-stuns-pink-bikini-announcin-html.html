Keyshia Cole stuns in pink bikini after 'announcing pregnancy'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Rapper Niko Khale, who has been dating singer and songwriter Keyshia Cole for a while, took to Instagram to share a photo of himself and his girlfriend on the beach.

The image was a selfie taken by Cole that showed her wearing a bright pink bikini that had a pineapple drawn on the bra. The 36-year-old singer was seriously looking at the camera while her boyfriend was right behind her.

Khale, who is 22 years old, was only wearing orange, red, and blue swimming trunks and a cap, what put his upper body on full display and proved hoy muscular he is.

Apart from that, Khale’s tattoos attracted a lot of sights as his chest and left arm are almost entirely covered by them.

For the caption, the rapper wrote that he and Cole were living ‘their best lives.’ As soon as the image was uploaded, his fans and followers took to the comment section to praise the couple and share their thoughts.

One of them was Instagram user @redbonebutterpecan, who congratulated the couple and added that Keisha, who has a big heart, deserves to be loved, not broken. User @kisosweet_ chimed in and said that Cole and Khale made a wonderful couple.

Earlier this month, Cole used her private Instagram account to let her fans know that she ‘was pregnant.’ Even though she didn’t say who the father is, everything suggested that Khale was the lucky one.

As Rap-Up reported, everything was a sort of joke as the singer later uploaded another photo apologizing for ‘trolling’ everybody with the fake pregnancy news. She added that people should stop body shamming her by saying that she is pregnant every other day.

Cole has an eight-year-old son, Daniel Gibson Jr., from a previous relationship she had with former NBA star Daniel Gibson. The couple was married from 2011 to 2017, but they announced their separation in 2014.
